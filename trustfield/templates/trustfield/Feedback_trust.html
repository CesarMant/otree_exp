Suppose that you sent 30 points to person B.
Having received the tripled amount (90 points), person B sent 50 points back to you.
In the end, how many points would you and person B have?

Solution: You would have 70 points and person B would have 40 points.

Explanation: You sent 30 points to person B. It was tripled thus person B received 30*3=90 points.
Then person B sent 50 points back to you. You received exactly 50 points.
Hence at last you would have 50-30+50=70 points, and person B would have 90-50=40 points.
