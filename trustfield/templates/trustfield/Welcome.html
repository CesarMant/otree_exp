Thank you for your participation.
This activity is part of a study to understand how people take decisions involving others.
By attending this invitation you have already earned 40 Yuan that you will get as your promised participation fee.
Moreover, you will be paid also for the activity in which you are about to participate.
You will be confronted with several decisions that involve real money.
How much you earn depends on the choices of you and other participants.

The other participants are in this same room taking part of this activity.
You will not know who the other participants are, and they will not know who you are.
Your choices and outcomes will be confidential.

This activity is divided in two blocks.

In the first block you will interact with another participant at a time.
This interaction will be repeated for five rounds with different participants each round.
At the end of the activity we will randomly select one of the rounds of first block to be paid.

In the second block you will interact with three other participants at a time.
This interaction will be repeated for six rounds with the same group of three participants every round.
At the end of the activity all the rounds in the second block will be paid.

You will be endowed with points to play.
Depending on your decisions you may earn, or lose, additional points.
At the end of the activity we will pay you for each point according to this exchange rate: 1 point = {{ exchange_rate }} Yuan.
We will finish by asking you some questions about yourself.
All the collected information will remain confidential and it will be used only with academic purposes.

Please do not talk to anyone during the activity.
