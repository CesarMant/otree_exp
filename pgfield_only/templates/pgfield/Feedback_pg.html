Suppose in a group during the first stage:
A participant contributed 10 points,
another participant contributed 8 points,
another participant contributed 6 points and
another participant contributed 1 point.

Aftwerwards, in the second stage: The participant who contributed 6 points spent one disapproval point.
One disapproval point is assigned to the participant who contributed 6 points.

What would be the payoff of the participant who contributed 6 points before and after the second stage?

Solution: 14 points before the second stage and 10 points after the second stage

Explanation:
Total contribution from all participants was 10+8+6+1=25. Total earnings from the project were 25 x 1.6=40.
Each participant got an equal share of the earnings, that is, 40/4=10.
Hence, the payoff for the participant who contributed 6 points before the second stage was 10-6+10=14.

The participant who contributed 6 points spent one of his points assigning a disapproval point to another participant.
One point is deducted from his payoff.
One disapproval point was assigned to the participant who contributed 6 points.
Three additional points are deducted from his payoff.
Hence, the payoff for the participant who contributed 6 points after the second stage was 14-1-3=10.
