Suppose that participant A sent 30 points to participant B.
Having received the tripled amount, participant B sent 50 points to participant A.
In the end, how many points would participant A and B have?

Solution: Participant A would have 70 points and Participant B would have 40 points.

Explanation: Participant A first sent 30 points to B. It was tripled thus B received 30*3=90 points.
Then participant B sent 50 points to A. Participant A received exactly 50 points.
Hence at last participant A would have 50-30+50=70 points, and participant B would have 90-50=40 points.
