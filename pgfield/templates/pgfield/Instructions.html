First Stage: Allocation to the public fund
You have been matched with three (3) other participants.
Each group member, including you, has received {{ Constants.endowment }}.
You decide how many of your {{ Constants.endowment }} you would like to allocate in the public fund.
That is completely of your choice.
Every point allocated to the public fund, either by you or by any other group member, will be multiplied by {{ Constants.effic_factor }}.
The points in the public fund will be divided equally among the {{ Constants.players_per_group }} group members.
Every group member gets from the public fund exactly the same number of points, no matter how many they allocate in this fund.

Second Stage: Allocation of Disapproval Cards
At the beginning of this stage you will see how much each of the other group members contributed to the public fund.
You decide whether you allocate a Disapproval Card to each group member or not.
Each Disapproval Card you allocate to others reduces your income in {{ Constants.punish_cost }} points.
Each Disapproval Card a group member receives will reduce his income in {{ Constants.punish_tech }} points.
The other group members can also allocate Disapproval Cards to you if the wish to.
For each group member you need to click on "Yes" or "No" for the allocation of the Disapproval Card.
The more Disapproval Cards you allocate, the higher your costs.
