Suppose that during the first stage:
You contributed 5 points.
The other three participants contributed 10, 8 and 1 points, respectively.

Aftwerwards, during the second stage: You allocate 1 Disapproval Card.
One Disapproval Card is assigned to you.

What would be your payoff before and after the second stage?

Solution: 17 points before the second stage and 10 points after the second stage

Explanation:
Total contribution from all participants was 10+8+5+1=24. Total earnings from the project were 24 x 2 = 48.
Each participant got an equal share of the earnings, that is, 48/4=12.
Hence, your payoff before the second stage was 10-5+12=17.

You allocated one Disapproval Card. It decreased your payoff by 2 points.
One Disapproval Card was allocated to you. It decreased your payoff by 5 points.
Hence, your payoff after the second stage was 17-2-5=10.
